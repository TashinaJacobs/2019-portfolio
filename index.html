A Little About Tashina

With a study background in graphic design and photography I wanted to build on my repertoire and learn more about web design, focusing on user centered design and using industry standard practices. I have loved creating and building my own websites from scratch and the user testing that gives you so much insight into the way the user experiences the product, I have learnt invaluable lessons on the importance of this throughout the design process and I look forward to bringing all of my knowledge and skills together to create, design and build beautiful websites and applications out there in the (real world) digital design industry.
